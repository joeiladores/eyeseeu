About Us

Routine Exam? Emergency Eye Problem? We're on it. Helping you achieve the best possible vision is why we're here. New patients are frequently surprised by our level of compassion. Long time patients can't imagine us being any other way. We listen. We ask questions. We communicate clearly. From front desk to our team rooms, you'll be heard and respected.

Meet our Eye Doctors and Opticians in the Philippines & The Surrounding Areas

When you come to Optometrists Clinic Inc, you gain access to a team of professional optometrists, opticians, and eye doctors in Edmonton. We are a very well-known and respected team with a unique skill set and specialty. Rest assured that whatever your optical needs may be, we have an eye doctor who has the education and experience to confidently meet your needs.

We are proud to employ competent and knowledgeable opticians and optometric assistants in order to ensure that your unique needs are met. Our team approach means that we can offer a wide variety of services. For example, our eye doctors perform general and pediatric exams, as well as care for a variety of ocular health problems. We are at the forefront of contact lens technology, and always endorse the newest lenses in the market to best meet our patient’s needs. When you come to one of our locations, we will diagnose and treat eye conditions such as glaucoma, cataracts, infections, allergies, and diabetes.

EyeSeeU Optical Team

Dr. Jeleena Denise Roldan, OD

CEO and Chief Optometrist

Dr. Jeleena received her Doctor of Optometry degree in 2006 from the University of the Philippines (Dean’s Honours List). At that time, she was also presented with the Award for Excellence in Patient Management by the Philippine Association of Optometrists for her exceptional patient care.

Dr. Juan Malaya, OD

Optometrist - Digos Branch

Dr. Malaya graduated from the University of Santo Tomas in 2003 with a Doctor of Optometry degree (Dean’s Honour List). He has been practicing in Digos City with the EyeSeeU Optical for his entire career. He has special interest in pediatrics, contact lenses and family practice.

Dr. Lukas Malakas, OD

Optometrist - Marbel Branch

Dr. Malakas received a B.Sc. in Laboratory Medicine at the University of Santo Tomas, and a Doctor of Optometry at the University of the Philippines. His optometric interests in family practice include ocular disease. Dr. Malakas was involved in research for a breast cancer antigen detection kit, and he also published and co-authored an article on the role of pathogenic yeast in humans.

Dr. Maria Katapang, OD

Optometrist - Isulan Branch

Dr. Katapang graduated from the University of the Philippines with a Bachelor of Science in 2015. She then went to University of Santo Tomas to complete her Doctorate of Optometry. She graduated with distinction in 2019. Dr. Katapang has a special interest in family practice, ocular disease, and contact lenses.
